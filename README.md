## 📌 Overview

A set of executive-ready financial and market performance reports built in Excel, designed to support strategic business decisions through clear data presentation and analysis.

## 📂 Reports Included

| Report | Description |
|---|---|
| 📊 **Customer Performance Report** | Customer-wise revenue breakdown, growth tracking, and performance rankings |
| 🌍 **Market Performance vs Target** | Regional sales vs targets with variance and gap analysis |
| 💰 **P&L by Fiscal Year** | Annual profit & loss with gross margin and net profit trends |
| 📅 **P&L by Months** | Month-by-month financial performance for seasonal analysis |
| 🗺️ **P&L by Markets** | Market-level profitability to identify high and low-performing regions |

## 💡 Business Value

- **Stakeholder-ready** PDF reports for executive presentations
- **Clear KPIs**: Net Sales, Gross Margin %, COGS, Net Profit
- **Trend identification** across fiscal years and market regions
- **Actionable insights** for pricing and market expansion strategy

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Microsoft Excel** — Advanced formulas, Pivot Tables, Charts
- **PDF Export** — Executive-ready formatted reports
